    Hello, I'm Abdullah Al Maini, a third-year Computer Science student currently residing in Mississauga, Ontario. I'm pursuing my Honours Bachelor of Science degree with a specialization in Computer Science, Mathematics, and Statistics at the University of Toronto, with expected graduation in May 2027.

    I have a passion for software development, machine learning, and problem-solving, which I've honed through various projects, coursework, and professional experience. Recently, I completed an internship at AIP Labs as a Machine Learning Engineer Intern, where I gained hands-on experience with cutting-edge AI technologies in the field of dermatology, including developing and refining deep learning models to achieve a 98% precision rate in image classification tasks.

    My technical expertise includes working with PyTorch, PyTorch Lightning, and Scikit-Learn for model development, as well as conducting data analysis using Pandas and NumPy. During my internship, I curated and analyzed over 200,000 medical images, trained deep neural networks, and refined the training pipeline, achieving a 1.5x improvement in precision and recall metrics across classes.

    With a strong foundation in languages like Java, Python, C, C++, C#, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and TypeScript, coupled with experience in machine learning frameworks, I'm continuously expanding my skill set. My projects range from developing a Receipt OCR application to creating an Academic Planner Full Stack Web Application where I led a team of 7 using Jira for agile project management.

    I'm proficient in Git version control and have experience deploying applications on platforms like Heroku. I believe in the power of collaboration and agile methodologies, which is evident in my exposure to Scrum during coursework and project management.

    Outside of academia and professional work, I enjoy MMA, video games, and exploring new technologies! Fluent in both English and Arabic, I'm excited about opportunities to connect with diverse communities and contribute meaningfully to the ever-evolving tech landscape.

    Welcome to my digital space, where I'll be sharing my journey, projects, and insights as I continue to grow and learn in the world of software development and machine learning.
