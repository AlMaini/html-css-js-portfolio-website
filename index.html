    Hello, I'm Abdullah Al Maini, a third-year Computer Science student currently residing in Toronto, Ontario. I'm pursuing my Bachelor of Science degree with honors in Computer Science, Mathematics, and Physics at the University of Toronto.

    I have a passion for software development, machine learning, and problem-solving, which I've honed through various projects, coursework, and professional experience. Recently, I completed an internship at AIP Labs as a Machine Learning Intern, where I gained hands-on experience with cutting-edge AI technologies in the field of dermatology, including developing and refining deep learning models to achieve a 98% precision rate in image classification tasks.

    With a strong foundation in languages like Java, Python, and C, coupled with experience in machine learning frameworks such as PyTorch and PyTesseract, I'm continuously expanding my skill set. One of my recent projects involved building a Receipt OCR application, where I utilized OpenCV and PyTesseract to automate text extraction from over 1,000 images, achieving a 90% accuracy rate. By integrating Large Language Models (LLMs) to correct textual errors, I enhanced the overall text recognition accuracy, particularly for complex alphanumeric data. Additionally, I implemented regular expressions to extract specific patterns, such as product names and prices, improving data validation and consistency.

    I'm proficient in Git version control and have experience deploying applications on platforms like Heroku. I believe in the power of collaboration and agile methodologies, which is evident in my exposure to Scrum during coursework and project management. My projects range from developing stock price prediction systems using Python and statistical models to crafting accessible-oriented adventure games in Java.

    Outside of academia and professional work, I enjoy MMA, video games, and exploring new technologies! Fluent in both English and Arabic, I'm excited about opportunities to connect with diverse communities and contribute meaningfully to the ever-evolving tech landscape.

    Welcome to my digital space, where I'll be sharing my journey, projects, and insights as I continue to grow and learn in the world of software development and machine learning.
